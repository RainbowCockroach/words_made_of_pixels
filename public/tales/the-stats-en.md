"Stats" has been there for a while. Some said it comes from "them" themselves, some said it's an invention of the gods. One determines their stats by taking tests at the shining box in the lobby. It's like a quiz of sort.

Those stats are:

- Sense: World building, or "physics" in the mortal ways. Do not underestimate the works required to create a self-sustaining universe from scratch. Rare to master. The ones who do, are either selling universes, sprinkling sense break to wreck other universes, or got murdered by Black Ring for selling universes

- Surreal: Imagination and the ability to manifest them. Easy to do, hard to master. Almost everyone can bend a spoon just by looking at them. To manifest a spoon out of thin air is another thing. Usually requires good amount of concentration. Sometimes a bit of shroom.

- Sane: Many calls them "HP bar". If a god ever felt helpless, they lose their power. Some minds are more fragile.

- Social: Influence. Charisma. See through your enemy's mind and break their sanity. More friend. Less enemy. Gather an army of simps ready to witch hunt and die in your stead.

- Speed: How to take down a powerful deity with your disappointing stats? Strike them before they can manifest their skills! Note: It's considered extremely rude to attack magical girls in the middle of transformation.
