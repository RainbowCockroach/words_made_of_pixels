Unable to bear the rotting smell in the kitchen, go take out the trash at 3 AM. Light drizzle. Toss the trash, suddenly I notice pale bare legs poking out from the trash bin.  
My neighbor, completely naked, lies like a rag doll in the trash bin. Lifeless eyes, but cheeks still rosy, not the color of a corpse. His chest rises and falls slightly. Still breathing, but no response when I shake and call him.  
Shuddering, I called the police. They came, shined their flashlights, examined for a long while. Then one officer sighed.

"This one needs the body cleanup crew"

\* \* \*

After many phone calls, a group of people in sanitation worker uniforms arrived, they casually pulled the neighbor out and tossed him in a large black bag. A strange young man was called over by the police.

"Is this your body?"  
"That's right, I just swapped bodies, that old body of mine I don't use anymore"  
"Bodies are considered special waste, you can't just throw it out like common household waste. Body waste must be collected at designated points. You should have known, this is in the handbook you were given when you immigrated."  
"Ah, sorry, I didn't pay attention" - the guy said embarrassedly.

This person paid a fine. He scratched his head and smiled awkwardly at me
"Sorry about that, startled you this late at night. My old body was quite aged, and also had diabetes. I just bought this new one. Cost a lot, but worth the money..."
